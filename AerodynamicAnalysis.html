Aerodynamic Analysis Techniques

Comprehensive approach to aerodynamic evaluation combining computational fluid dynamics, experimental testing, and performance optimization strategies.

CFD Simulations: Computational fluid dynamics analysis using advanced turbulence models and mesh optimization techniques.
Wind Tunnel Testing: Experimental validation through controlled wind tunnel environments with force measurement and flow visualization.
Performance Optimization: Multi-objective optimization algorithms to balance aerodynamic efficiency with practical design constraints.
Data Analysis & Validation: Statistical analysis and uncertainty quantification to ensure reliable aerodynamic predictions.
Computational Fluid Dynamics

Utilizing state-of-the-art CFD software to simulate complex flow phenomena, including turbulence, separation, and compressible effects in high-speed flows.

Software: Siemens Star-CCM+, ANSYS Fluent, OpenFOAM with LES and RANS turbulence models.

Experimental Aerodynamics

Conducting systematic wind tunnel testing with advanced measurement techniques including pressure taps, force balances, and PIV flow visualization.

Capabilities: Subsonic and transonic testing, model design, and data acquisition systems.

Aerodynamic Optimization

Applying optimization algorithms and design of experiments methodologies to achieve optimal aerodynamic performance within design constraints.

Methods: Response surface methodology, genetic algorithms, and adjoint-based optimization.
